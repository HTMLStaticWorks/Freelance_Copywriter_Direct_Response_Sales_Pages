Behind the Conversions

I'm Alex Carter. I've spent the last 8 years figuring out exactly what makes people open their wallets online.

From broke agency writer to highly-paid mercenary.

Back in 2018, I was working at a "top" marketing agency writing SEO blog posts that nobody read. I was bored, broke, and realized something important: Traffic means nothing if you can't convert it.

I quit, bought every book by Gary Halbert, Eugene Schwartz, and Dan Kennedy, and locked myself in my apartment for 6 months. I started writing sales pages for local businesses on performance. If it didn't sell, I didn't eat.

Fast forward to today, my copy has generated over $25M in verified client revenue. I don't write "cute" branding copy. I write words that sell.

My Arsenal
